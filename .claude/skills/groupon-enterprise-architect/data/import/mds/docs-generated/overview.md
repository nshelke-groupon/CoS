# Marketing Deal Service (MDS) Overview

## Purpose

Marketing Deal Service (MDS) is the central deal data management, enrichment, and distribution service within the Groupon Continuum platform. It aggregates deal information from multiple upstream sources (Deal Catalog, Deal Management API, Pricing, Merchant/Places, Geo, and CRM), enriches it with taxonomy, location, margin, and performance data, and distributes processed deal data to downstream consumer channels, partner feeds, and the Marketing Platform. MDS serves as the authoritative hub for marketing-ready deal representations used across advertising, sponsored placements, SEM, and internal reporting.

## Scope

### In scope

- Deal event consumption from the shared message bus and Deal Catalog Service
- Multi-step deal enrichment pipeline (taxonomy, catalog, margin, performance, location)
- Inventory status aggregation across five domain-specific inventory services (Voucher, Goods, Third-Party, Travel, GLive)
- Feed generation for partner and consumer channels
- Performance metric aggregation and merchant KPI reporting
- CRM/Salesforce attribute enrichment and synchronization
- Deal change notification and publishing to the Marketing Platform
- Distributed locking, retry scheduling, and queue-based processing via Redis

### Out of scope

- Deal creation and lifecycle management (owned by `continuumDealManagementApi`)
- Deal catalog storage and taxonomy hierarchy (owned by `continuumDealCatalogService`)
- Pricing rule calculation (owned by `continuumPricingService`)
- Inventory fulfillment and redemption (owned by respective inventory services)
- Consumer-facing deal browsing and search (owned by MBNXT/frontend)
- Order processing and payment capture (owned by `continuumOrdersService`)
