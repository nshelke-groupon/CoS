# Invoice Management (IAS) Overview

## Purpose

invoice_management (also known as IAS — Invoice Aggregator Service) is the central invoicing and payment processing service for Groupon's Goods platform. It aggregates purchase order events, shipment events, and sales order updates from the Goods message bus into invoices, transmits those invoices to NetSuite for financial accounting, and manages the full payment reconciliation lifecycle. The service also handles vendor receiver creation, remittance report generation, and 3PL (third-party logistics) invoice generation.

## Scope

### In scope

- Invoice creation from Purchase Order (PO) events
- Marketplace invoice creation from GMO (goods marketplace item shipped) events
- Payment fetch and reconciliation against outstanding invoices
- Invoice transmission to NetSuite via OAuth 1.0a
- NetSuite callback (webhook) handling for payment status updates
- Receiver creation from Receiver events
- Remittance report generation and upload to AWS S3
- 3PL invoice generation for third-party logistics partners
- Purchase order management and listing
- Shipment status tracking via Shipment Tracker integration
- Payment details export
- Vendor management (receiver and supplier data)
- Scheduled job orchestration via Quartz Scheduler

### Out of scope

- Goods order placement (handled by Commerce Interface / GPAPI)
- Shipment tracking data ownership (sourced from Shipment Tracker)
- NetSuite master data management (NetSuite is the system of record for accounting)
- Email delivery infrastructure (delegated to Rocketman)
- Deal catalog and pricing (sourced from Goods Stores API)
