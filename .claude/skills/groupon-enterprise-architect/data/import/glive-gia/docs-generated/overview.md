# GrouponLive Inventory Admin (GIA) Overview

## Purpose

GIA (GrouponLive Inventory Admin) is the internal administrative web application that manages the full lifecycle of live event deals on the GrouponLive platform. It handles deal creation and state transitions, event and inventory management for ticketing partners (Ticketmaster, Provenue, AXS), invoice generation, and merchant payment processing. GIA acts as the central operational hub through which GrouponLive staff configure, monitor, and settle live event inventory sourced from third-party ticketing systems.

## Scope

### In scope

- Creating, editing, and transitioning deals through their publishing lifecycle
- Managing events and options attached to live event deals
- Bulk-updating event inventory from external ticketing providers
- Configuring per-deal settings for Ticketmaster, Provenue, and AXS integrations
- Generating invoices for GrouponLive deals and tracking payment status
- Detecting and reporting uninvoiced deals requiring action
- Managing user accounts and role-based access control within GIA
- Syncing deal data from Salesforce contracts
- Generating merchant reports
- Managing redemption codes per deal

### Out of scope

- Consumer-facing deal discovery or purchase (handled by MBNXT / storefront)
- Order processing and fulfilment (handled by Continuum commerce engine)
- Raw ticketing inventory allocation at the ticketing-system level (owned by Ticketmaster/Provenue/AXS systems)
- Tax calculation (delegated to GTX service)
- Accounting ledger management (delegated to `continuumAccountingService`)
